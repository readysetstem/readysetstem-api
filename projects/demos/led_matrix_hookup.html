Hooking up LED Matrices

Need:

  • At least 1 LED matrix module
  • 6 male to female jumper wires
  • 1 male to male jumper wire

Steps:

  1. If you have more than 1 LED matrix, connect the LED matrices together using the 6 pins from one matrix into the socket of another.
  2. Place the LED matrix modules on the breadboard, using the 4 pins of each matrix as support.
  3. Using the 6 male to female jumper wires, connect the 6 pins of the first matrix to the correct ports of the Raspberry Pi.
  4. Using the male to male jumper wire, connect the 3 left most socket of the last matrix to the 9/MISO port on the Raspberry Pi.
