First, select the CREATOR lid and connect it to the base:

The CREATOR Lid is a blank breadboard that allows you to develop your own projects.

Then, select your project:

Bouncing Dot

Hook up an LED matrix cell and then show a bouncing dot.

Balancing Dot

Hook up an LED matrix cell and an accelerometer cell and then balance a dot on the LED matrix.

Moving Dot

Hook up an LED matrix cell and 4 tactile buttons and then move a dot on the LED matrix with the buttons.
