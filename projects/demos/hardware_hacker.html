First, select the CREATOR lid and connect it to the base:

The CREATOR Lid is a blank breadboard that allows you to develop your own projects.

Then, select your project:

LED Matrix Cell

Hook up an LED matrix cell and display an LED animation.

Accelerometer Cell

Hook up an accelerometer cell and display a balanced ball on the computer screen.

Button Cell

Hoop up 2 tactile buttons and then control a ball on the computer screen.
